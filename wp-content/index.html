Greetings!

At Franklin Massage Center our mission is to offer high-quality, results-focused,
personalized care for each person we treat. Our talented Licensed Massage
Therapists specialize in Deep Tissue Massage as well as a range of modalities
that can address the needs of aspiring and professional athletes, clients with
chronic pain, people seeking to reduce stress and fatigue, and more. While our
team provides a diverse array of skills, we are united in our efforts to get to the
root cause of your issues, bring relief, and facilitate true and lasting change. Our
thorough and compassionate care got us voted “Best Massage” three years in a
row in the Nashville Scene’s Best of Nashville.

We consider it an honor to collaborate with our community partners like Fleet
Feet, Nolensville Running Club and more and are thrilled to be working closely
with the staff at Axis Chiropractic as well as top physical therapists, personal
trainers, and orthopedic doctors in town. Whether you are beginning to train as a
runner in a Fleet Feet training program or you are a champion ironman, we are
committed to delivering you with the highest level of care for your body.
